My Favorite Movie: The Prestige

Let's talk about my current favorite movie, The Prestige, directed by Christopher Nolan starring Hugh Jackman and Christian Bale.

From the beginning or first 15 minutes there were two main characters, Angier and Borden. At the first scene, Borden was imprisoned because he "killed" Angier. Then a classic Christopher Nolan blocking is illustrated which involves different timelines of each scenario. It was later revealed in the movie that the root of Angier and Borden's rivalry was because Borden allegedly tried a different tie of knot to Angier's wife, who was Angier's assistant magician as well. I was not expecting the plot twist at the end, making it one of my favorite movies today.
